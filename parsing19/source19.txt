易太极十九式口诀

1. 起势 Beginning form

Stand with feet together and hands at the outside of the thighs.
双腿并立，双手放在双腿两侧。

Shift the weight center to the right leg, bend the right leg and sink, lift the left knee up and step to the left, landing the toes and then the whole foot.
重心到右腿，右腿慢慢往下沉 ，左腿左膝领劲慢慢往上提，向左横开一步，点地，踏实。

Stand up slowly and shift the weight center to the middle.
右脚慢慢站立，移重心到中间。
  
Draw the abdomen in, sink , expand the chest.
收腹，下沉，胸腔膨胀。

Continue to sink and drop the hands down to the outside of the knees.
继续下沉，带双手下落于双膝外侧。

Draw the abdomen in, sink, chest in, and turn both arms inward.
收腹，下沉，含胸，带双臂内旋。

Continue to sink, relax the shoulders and elbows.
继续下沉，松肩，松肘。

Draw the abdomen in, sink, expand the chest. Lift up the hands slowly in front of the body.
收腹，下沉，胸腔膨胀，双手在身体前方慢慢往上提。

Draw the shoulders backward, sink, continue to sink, relax the shoulders and elbows. 
双肩向后，下沉，继续下沉，松肩，松肘。

Lean slightly forward.
微前倾。

Sink, and drop the hands down.
下沉，带双手下落。


2.金刚捣碓Jin Gang Dao Dui

Draw abdomen in, sink, drop the hands, draw the chest in, and turn the left arm inward and the right hand outward to the center.
收腹，下沉，双手下落，含胸，左手臂内旋,右手外旋向左划弧。

Sink, turn the body slightly to the right, and turn both hands.
下沉，微右转，双手翻掌。

Sink, turn slightly to the left and shift the weight center to the right, and ward off to the left.
下沉，微左转移重心到右腿，双手向左方掤出。

Sink and raise the hands up, relax the left shoulder, elbow, lift up the right forearm, turn both hands.
上下对开，左手松肩、松肘，右小臂上抬，双手翻掌。

Turn slightly to the right, shift the weight center to the left, and continue to turn both hands and swing to the right.
微右转，移重心到左腿，双手继续翻掌，向右划弧。
 
Sink, turn to the right, and turn the right toes outward.
下沉，右转，摆右脚尖。

Draw the abdomen in, sink, expand the chest, and draw both hands backward slightly.收腹，下沉，胸腔膨胀,两手微内收。

Draw the chest in, separate the elbows slightly.
含胸，双肘微打开。

Sink, relax the back of the body.
下沉，后背放松。

Sink, lean forward, and draw both hands backward slightly.
下沉，上身前倾，双手微内收。

Sink, shift the weight center to the right, and push both hands forward.
下沉，移重心到右腿，双手前推。

Continue to sink, draw in the left foot, and push both hands forward opposite with the body
.继续下沉，收左腿，双手前推与身体对开。

Draw the right hip inward, move hands opposite with the body and draw in the left foot.
收右胯，手与身体对开，收左腿。

Sink, lift the left foot and step 45 degrees to the left of the back.
下沉，提左腿向左后45°铲出。

Sink and raise the hands, lift the left forearm up.
上下对开，左小臂上抬。

Turn to the right, draw the right hand backward, and turn the left hand inward.
右转， 右手向后抽，左手内旋。

Sink, turn the left hand inward, the right hand outward and push, palms down.
下沉，左手内旋、右手外旋前推，翻掌向下。

Sink, and press the hands downward.
下沉，带双手下按。

Turn the body slightly to the right, shift the weight center to the left, turn the body to the left, relax and sink when the body is turning back, turn the right hand, palm up.
微右转，移重心到左腿，身体左转，至身体回正时放松下沉，翻右手，掌心向上。

Draw the abdomen in and sink, draw the chest in, and separate the elbows. 
收腹下沉，含胸，双肘打开。

Part the hands.
双手向两侧打开。

Relax the back of the body, continue to part the hands.
后背放松，双手继续向两侧打开。

Sink, turn the body to the left, turn the right hand inward, palm to the front and down to the outside of the right thigh, draw the left hand slightly inward.
下沉，身体左转，右手内旋至右手掌心向前，下落于右大腿外侧，左手微内收。

Lift the right foot and step forward, move the right hand forward, left fingers on the lower part of the right upper arms.
提右腿上步，带右手向前，左手合于右大臂下方。

Sink, lift the right forearm up.
下沉，右小臂向上抬。

Lean slightly forward, turn the left palm up, close to the body, draw in the right hand.
微前倾，左手贴身翻掌，右手微里合。

Sink, relax both hands, drop them downward, and draw back the right foot.
下沉，双手放松下落，收右脚。

Lift the right knee and right fist up.
提右膝，右手由掌变拳上提。

Stomp the right foot and punch.
震脚砸拳。

3. 懒扎衣Lan Zha Yi
         
Draw the abdomen in, chest in, and turn the elbows outward.
收腹下沉，含胸，双肘掤出。

Turn the elbows inward, turn slightly to the right, shift the weight center to the left, move both hands to the right of the front.
双肘里合，右转移重心到左腿，双手向右前方掤出。

Continue turning the elbows inward, sink, turn to the left, shift the weight center to the right, and drop the hands before the left hip.
双肘继续里合，下沉，左转，移重心到右腿，双手下落贴于左胯前。

Draw the right hip inward, chest in, rotate both elbows outward.
收右胯，含胸，双肘向外旋出。

Rotate both arms inward, sink, lift the right elbow up, thread the left hand down with the palm facing backward.
双臂内旋，人下沉，右肘向上提，左掌心向内贴大腿下穿。

Sink, turn to the right and shift the weight center to the left, push the right fist outward.
下沉，右转移重心到左腿，右拳外推。

Turn slightly to the left, thread the left hand down to the left, meanwhile, rotate the right arm outward.
微左转，左掌往左下方穿掌同时右手臂外旋。

Turn to the right, rotate the left arm outward and move it to the right in a curve, continue to rotate the right hand outward.
右转，左臂外旋向右划弧，右手继续外旋。

Turn to the left, body back to the front, move the right hand to the left, cross the hands, sink.
左转，身体回正，带右拳向左，双手相合，下沉。

Continue to sink, thread the right hand, drop the left hand downward, turn to the left and shift the weight center to the right.
继续下沉，穿右掌，左掌笔直下落，左转移，重心到右腿。

Draw the right hip in, rotate the right hand, turn the right hand; rotate the left hand, palm back, turn slightly to the right , part the right hand, turn the body back to the front.
收右胯，右手内旋，翻右掌；左手内旋至掌心向后，微右转，右手拉开，身体回正。

Sink, draw the right forearm in.
人下沉，收右小臂。

Draw the right hand backward, turn to the right and shift the weight center to the left.
右手向后抽，右转移重心到左腿。

Turn slightly to the left, thread the left hand to the left, palm back, turn the body back to the front and push the right hand out.
微左转，左手掌心向后向左穿掌，身体回正，右手外推。

Sink, turn slightly to the right, rotate both hands outward, move the left hand to the front.
下沉，微右转，双手外旋翻掌，带左手向前。

Draw the right hip in, sink, rotate the right hand, palm to the front.
收右胯，下沉，右手内旋至掌心向前。

Sink, turn slightly to the left, draw the right foot in, drop the right hand; draw the left forearm in.
下沉，微左转，收右腿，右手下落；左小臂内收。

Sink, expand the chest, rotate the right hand slightly outward, and draw the left forearm in.
下沉，开胸，右手微外旋，左小臂内收。

Sink, lift the right foot and step to the right, cross the hands at the inner part of the right knee.
下沉，提右腿向右铲出，双手合于右膝内侧。

Sink, turn to the left, shift the weight center to the right, turn the left heel inward, move the hands before the left hip.
下沉，左转，移重心到右腿，左脚跟内收，两手落于左胯前。

Sink and raise the hands, lift the right forearm, drop the left elbow slightly.
上下对开，收右小臂，同时左肘微下落。

Sink, turn the right hand, palm 45 degrees.
下沉，右手翻掌至掌心斜下45°。

Push the right hand , palm facing outward .
左手推右手至右手掌心向外。

Sink, push the right hand forward, press the left hand down to the abdomen.
人下沉，右手前推，左手下按至腹前。

Sink, turn the body to the right, back to the front, relax the right shoulder, lower the elbow, turn outward, sink, meanwhile turn the left hand.
下沉，右转，身体回正，右手松肩、沉肘，外旋，下沉，同时翻左掌。

4. 上步斜行Shang Bu Xie Xing
Sink, turn the left toes outward, turn to the left, and rotate the right hand outward.
下沉，摆左脚尖，左转，右手外旋。

Sink, turn to the left, thread the left hand to the right, continue to rotate the right hand outward.
下沉，左转，左掌向右手方向穿，右掌继续外旋。

Continue to turn to the left, rotate the left hand inward, and the right hand outward.
继续左转，左手内旋，右手继续外旋。

Turn slightly to the left, lift the right foot and step forward, move the hands upward, forward, then sink and press down. 
微左转，提右腿上步，带双手向上、向前，下沉，双手下打。

Draw the abdomen in and sink, drop both hands to the thighs, expand the chest, lift the hands up to the hips and sink, draw the chest slightly in, separate the elbows aside meanwhile turn the right toes outward. 
收腹下沉，双手下落贴大腿，胸腔膨胀，带双手向上提至掌跟贴于双胯前下沉，微含胸，两肘向身体两侧打开，同时摆右脚尖。

Turn the body to the right, lean forward and shift the weight center to the right.
右转，前倾，移重心到右腿。

Sink, draw the left foot in, meanwhile relax and drop the hands before the knees.
下沉，收左腿，同时两手放松下落于两膝前。

Stand up slightly, turn the body to the left, rotate the left hand inward and the right hand outward.
身体微站起，左转，左手内旋，右手外旋。
 
Sink, turn the body to the right, move hands up to the left in a curve, meanwhile, lift the left foot up.
下沉，右转，两手向左上划弧，同时提左腿。

Sink, lift the left foot and step 45 degrees to the left, hands ward off to the right of the front.
下沉，提左腿向左侧45°铲出，双手掤于右前方。

Sink and raise the hands, turn slightly to the right and turn the hands.
上下对开，微右转，翻掌。

Sink , draw the right forearm slightly in, press with the left hand.
下沉，右小臂微里收，左手向下按。

Draw the abdomen in, sink, thread with the right hand, turn to the left and bring the left hand to the inner part of the left knee.
收腹，下沉，右掌微前穿，左转带左手于左膝内侧。

Sink, rotate the left hand inward and fingers pointing to the right, shift the weight center to the left, draw the right hand in, move the left hand forward till the palm close to the inner part of the left knee.
下沉，左手内旋至指尖向里，移重心到左腿，右手内收，左手前移至掌根贴左膝内侧。

Sink, relax the left shoulder, elbow, turn the body to the left, move the left hand around the left knee, move the right hand to the center.
下沉，左手松肩、松肘，左转，左手沿左膝盖绕圈，右手向身体中线划弧。

Sink, turn left hand to hook, move body upright, meanwhile, push the right hand to the front of the left shoulder.
下沉，左手变勾手，身体回正，同时右手推至左肩前。

Draw the right hip in, sink, draw chest in, rotate the hands inward.
收右胯，下沉，含胸，双手内旋。

Move the body upright, lift the left hand up and push the right hand forward.
上身回正，提左手，右手前推。

Sink, turn the body to the right, part the right hand, sink, rotate the right arm outward, relax the shoulder and elbow.
下沉，右转，右手拉开，人下沉，右手臂外旋，松肩、松肘。

5.初收 Chu Shou

下沉，收腹，含胸，双手内旋，微右转，带左手至右手处，双手背相合。

Sink, relax the shoulders and elbows.
下沉，松肩、松肘。

Continue to rotate both hands outward, turn the body slightly to the left, drop the hands meanwhile expand the chest.
双手继续外旋，微左转，双手下落同时胸腔膨胀。

Draw the abdomen in, chest in, and part the elbows slightly.
收腹下沉，含胸，两肘微打开。

Continue to sink, thread both hands downward.
继续下沉，双手向下插。

Draw the chest in, hold the hands to dig.
含胸，双手变挖。

人起来，带双手上提。

下沉，左转（右肘里合），双手外旋，移重心到右腿。

Move the hip out, drop the hands.
顶胯，双手下落。

收胯，微含胸，下沉，双手向上划弧。

左脚点地，双手上提到左前方45°。

人下沉，双手向右下方落，同时收左腿，胸腔膨胀，右手臂外旋。

下沉，微左转，左手内旋，右手外旋。

下沉，右转，左手内旋，右手外旋，向右向上划弧。

下沉，收左胯，左转，提左腿摆左脚尖，左手外旋外翻；右手里合内旋。

收腹，下沉，微左转，右手搭于左手腕。

右手推左手，左转移重心到左腿。

下沉，继续左转，左手外旋，向左向上、内旋，向右划弧，同时收右腿。

Sink, rotate the left hand inward, move up, and the right hand outward to the right, lift up the right leg. Move the hands in a curve, move the hands downward and then forward, meanwhile step back.
人下沉，左手内旋，向上，右手外旋向右，提右腿。双手再向下划弧前伸，同时向后撤步。

上下对开，收右小臂。

下沉，翻右掌。

收腹，下沉，右手翻掌，左手推右手至右手掌心向外。

下沉，移重心到右腿，左手内旋外撑，身体右转，双手拉开，下沉双臂微外旋，人下沉。

Continue to sink, relax the shoulders and elbows, expand the chest, part the elbows aside, fingers pointing each other before the chest. 
继续下沉，松肩，松肘，开胸，两肘向两侧打开，双手指尖相对至胸前。

Continue to sink press both hands down, stand up, continue to press the hands before the abdomen.
继续下沉带双手下按，人起来，双手继续向下按至腹前。

Draw the abdomen in, sink, drop the hands down, palms facing back, expand the chest, part the hands aside.
收腹，下沉，双手下落至掌心向里，胸腔膨胀，双手向两侧拉开。

Shift the weight center to the right, sink, chest in, move the hands forward in a curve, relax and close the hands slowly to the abdomen.
移重心到右腿，下沉，含胸，双手向前划弧，收左脚，放松，两手慢慢收拢至小腹部。

Stand up slowly, move the hands naturally at the sides and relax.
人慢慢站起来，双手自然回于身体两侧放松。
