
1. 起势 Beginning form

Stand with feet together and hands at the outside of the thighs.
双腿并立，双手放在双腿两侧。

Shift the weight center to the right leg, bend the right leg and sink, lift the left knee up and step to the left, landing the toes and then the whole foot.
重心到右腿，右腿慢慢往下沉 ，左腿左膝领劲慢慢往上提，向左横开一步，点地，踏实。
